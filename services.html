Project Leadership & Services

Transforming Construction Complexity into Community Impact

A capital project is a generational opportunity, but the path from vision to reality is often paved with technical hurdles and high-stakes financial decisions. Synchronic Services carries the technical weight of the building process so leadership can remain focused on their mission. As a professional extension of the organization’s team, the firm provides the independent advocacy required to manage risk, control costs, and ensure every dollar is mission-critical.

Our Approach

Phase 1

Strategic Definition & Mission-Aligned Planning

The most consequential financial decisions happen in the "Conception Phase." Synchronic Services ensures these early choices are rooted in data and aligned with the organization's long-term goals.

  • Needs Assessment: Defining the "why" and "how" of a space before the first drawing begins to ensure the program drives the architecture.
  • Operational Alignment: Ensuring the layout reflects actual workflows and community needs, prioritizing functionality over aesthetics.
  • Truth-In-Budgeting: Creating realistic cost models that include "hidden" expenses—permits, fees, and furnishings—that often surprise boards later.
Phase 2

Specialized Support for Funding & Grants

Securing funding requires proving that a project is feasible and professionally managed. The firm provides the technical validation that donors, lenders, and grant-makers require to build trust.

  • Washington State Grant Expertise: Specialized experience navigating complex funding streams, including DOC, EDI, and Solar grants.
  • Feasibility & Due Diligence: Conducting site assessments and zoning reviews to validate a project before significant capital is committed.
  • Technical Narratives: Translating complex construction details into compelling language for capital campaigns and grant applications.
Phase 3

Design Advocacy & Smart Stewardship

During the design phase, Synchronic Services acts as a bridge between architectural creativity and financial reality, ensuring the organization's interests are protected at every turn.

  • Value Engineering: Analyzing design choices for Tenant Improvements and Core & Shell renovations to find high-impact, low-maintenance alternatives.
  • Constructability Reviews: Catching errors and omissions on paper before they become expensive change orders in the field.
  • Team Selection & Vetting: Assisting in the procurement of architects, engineers, and consultants who value community impact.
Phase 4

Construction Oversight & Financial Defense

Once construction begins, the firm serves as the "eyes and ears" on-site, providing rigorous accountability and protecting the project budget.

  • On-Site Representation: Regular quality control visits to ensure the work matches the approved vision and contract specifications.
  • Budget & Schedule Defense: Rigorous monitoring of contractor pay applications and change orders to prevent budget creep.
  • Centralized Communication: Acting as the single point of contact for the contractor and design team, shielding executive leadership from daily construction friction.
Phase 5

Closeout & Operational Handover

A project is only successful when the doors open seamlessly. Synchronic Services manages the transition from a construction site to a functional community asset.

  • Punch List Management: Oversight of final details and equipment commissioning to ensure everything is operational from day one.
  • Seamless Handover: Ensuring the organization has all necessary documentation, warranties, and training to operate the new facility sustainably.

Your Partner in Success

A capital project should be a milestone for an organization, not a burden. Whether navigating the early planning stages or requiring oversight through the construction phase, Synchronic Services is dedicated to delivering sustainable, impactful results that honor the mission and the community.
