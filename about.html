Our Mission

Synchronic Services empowers nonprofits and community impact projects to navigate construction complexity with confidence. By bridging the gap between technical expertise and mission-driven vision, we provide the independent advocacy needed to transform ambitious goals into sustainable community assets.

About Shannon McIntyre

Founder & Project Manager, Synchronic Services

With a professional background spanning architecture, general contracting, and nonprofit leadership, I founded Synchronic Services to fill a critical gap in the building industry. Too often, visionary organizations are forced to choose between managing their mission and managing a construction site. I believe you should be able to do both.

My commitment to this work is deeply personal. Having served as a nonprofit manager and a long-standing board member, I understand that for community-impact organizations, a capital project is more than just a building—it is a generational investment and an act of stewardship. I’ve sat on your side of the table, and I’ve seen how technical hurdles, complex contracts, and fundraising pressures can bury even the most inspired leadership teams.

At Synchronic Services, I provide expert Owner’s Representation and Project Leadership throughout the Puget Sound region. I specialize in the technical and logistical nuances of Tenant Improvements and Core & Shell renovations, ensuring that your physical space is as high-performing as your programs. My goal is to act as your independent advocate, translating the complexities of the construction world into clear, actionable data.

By aligning technical expertise with strategic timing and coordination—the core of synchronicity—I ensure that every project reflects your organization’s values, protects your budget, and stays focused on the "Main Thing": the community you serve.
